Who We Are

Integrated Logistics Executed with Precision

NEXORA Shipping Pvt Ltd is a professionally managed logistics company headquartered in Chennai, India. We provide end-to-end freight management services across air, ocean, and inland transport — delivering reliability, speed, and strategic oversight to businesses of every scale.

Operating as a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC), NEXORA coordinates international shipments with authority, leveraging strong relationships with shipping lines, airlines, and regulatory bodies to ensure seamless cargo movement across global trade lanes.

Our team brings deep domain expertise, a compliance-first approach, and a client-centric culture to every consignment — from a single parcel to oversized project cargo.
