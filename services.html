Our Services

NEXORA delivers a fully integrated suite of logistics services designed to cover every stage of your cargo's journey — from origin to final destination.

Capabilities

Comprehensive Logistics Solutions

Each service is designed to integrate seamlessly within your supply chain, ensuring end-to-end execution with minimal complexity.

NVOCC Operations

NEXORA operates as a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC), issuing its own Bills of Lading and managing container space across global shipping lines.

  • House Bill of Lading issuance
  • FCL & LCL container management
  • Rate negotiation with mainline carriers
  • Space guaranteed on key trade routes
  • Cargo consolidation services

Air & Ocean Freight

NEXORA provides comprehensive air and ocean freight forwarding solutions for time-sensitive and volume shipments.

  • Air freight: express & economy
  • Ocean freight: FCL & LCL
  • Multi-modal shipment planning
  • Full cargo tracking & visibility
  • Export-import documentation management

Warehousing

NEXORA provides secure and strategically located warehousing facilities to support import, export, and domestic logistics operations.

  • Short and long-term storage
  • Bonded & non-bonded warehousing
  • Inventory tracking systems
  • Temperature-controlled options
  • Pick, pack & distribution services

Project Cargo

For oversized, heavy, or unconventional cargo, NEXORA provides specialized project cargo management.

  • Heavy lift & out-of-gauge cargo
  • Route survey & feasibility studies
  • Special equipment & vessel chartering
  • Permit handling & escort coordination
  • Engineering support & risk mitigation

Customs Clearance

NEXORA provides efficient, compliant customs clearance for both imports and exports.

  • Import & export customs filing
  • HS code classification
  • Duty calculation & drawback
  • DGFT & regulatory compliance
  • Examination & inspection coordination

Inland Transportation

NEXORA provides reliable and efficient inland transportation services to support first-mile and last-mile logistics.

  • Port-to-warehouse & door-to-door
  • FCL container movement
  • Multi-axle & specialised vehicles
  • GPS-tracked fleet operations
  • Pan-India coverage
